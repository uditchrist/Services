About us


Pinnacle First is an independent technical Inspection and Expediting Agency, based in Bangaluru, India. Pinnacle First was established in June 2018 with the strategic goals of providing Quality Assurance, Inspection and Expediting Services to our clients built upon the fundamental principles of delivering total project support and integration, achieving highest standards of management and contractor quality and promoting the most cost effective service possible. We provide our services all over India. We ensure our employees are fully trained to work as per requirement.

OUR SERVICES

INSPECTION

Delivering high quality and sophisticated products requires full control of processes and performances. Our inspector continuously trained to attend process such as welding, NDT, coating and any testing activity. Pinnacle will ensure you get what you expect. Our Inspectors ensure by appropriate checks and tests, that the products ordered by our clients are manufactured and supplied strictly in accordance with the specified requirements and applicable codes and practices.

EXPIDITING

Time is essential. As we raise expectations, managing a critical delay can be crucial for the success of a project. The Expediter proactively engages with the Vendor to determine and verify that manufacturing and documental progress expected are achieved; when a delay occurs he drives the Vendor to a mitigation plan. Pinnacle will keep the supplier's progress under control. Our service definition is that our expeditors comprehensively evaluate the supplier's production activities to ensure that our client delivery requirements will be achieved, and to assist in identifying potential problems or delays in the process at the earliest possible point with a view to establishing remedial actions with the supplier to return any slippage back to program.
