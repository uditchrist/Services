Nieuwe Parklaan 128 2587 BW Den Haag The Netherlands Office *31 70 2000 195 Mobile * 31 6 435 72 996 rene.cardinaal@cardinex.nl www.cardinex.nl Cardinex BV Inspections & Expediting Services Who we are: Cardinex BV is an independent technical Inspection and Expediting Agency, based in The Hague, The Netherlands. Cardinex BV was established in January 2013 with the strategic goals of providing Quality Assurance, Inspection and Expediting Services to our clients built upon the fundamental principles of delivering total project support and integration, achieving highest standards of management and contractor quality, and promoting the most cost effective service possible. Cardinex BV services are available throughout Europe, the Middle & Far East, Asia, and North America, and are built upon a centrally controlled network of over 150 suitably qualified and experienced employees and freelance inspectors, strategically located close to the major industrial centres to minimise travel costs, and to combine the advantages of full knowledge of local suppliers and language, to the benefit of all. Our employees are constantly trained to work according to the requirements of many National and International Standards. Cardinex BV ensures that the employees meet up-to-date in Terms of International Standards such as: ANSI, API, ASME, ASTM, BS, EN, ISO, NEN and PED.
